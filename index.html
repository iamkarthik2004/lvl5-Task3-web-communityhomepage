About GOVT H.S Manjoor

GOVT H.S Manjoor, established in 1908, is a historic educational institution managed by the Department of Education. Located in the serene rural area of KURAVILANGADU block in Kottayam district, Kerala, the school has been dedicated to providing quality education for over a century.

The school offers classes from Grade 1 to Grade 10 and follows a co-educational system. However, it does not have an attached pre-primary section.

With a strong emphasis on academic excellence and holistic development, GOVT H.S Manjoor continues to shape the future of its students by fostering knowledge, discipline, and values.

  • Academic Excellence and Innovation
  • Discipline, Integrity, and Responsibility
  • Respect for Diversity and Inclusion
  • Holistic Development through Co-curricular Activities
